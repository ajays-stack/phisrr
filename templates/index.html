Why Phishing Detection Matters

Phishing attacks are one of the most common and dangerous cyber threats today. They can lead to identity theft, financial loss, and unauthorized access to sensitive information. Our detection system helps protect you by analyzing URLs and identifying potential threats before you interact with them.

How It Works

  • Feature Extraction: The system extracts multiple features from the URL and webpage content to analyze its legitimacy.
  • Machine Learning Models: Advanced models trained on large datasets classify URLs as safe or phishing.
  • Real-time Feedback: Users get instant results to make informed decisions about website safety.

Tips to Avoid Phishing

  • Always verify the URL before clicking on links in emails or messages.
  • Look for HTTPS and valid security certificates on websites.
  • Be cautious of unsolicited requests for personal information.
  • Keep your software and antivirus updated regularly.
